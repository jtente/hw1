Upscale Fare

Al Forno

Located on the Harbor, this Providence staple has been garnering praise from food critics since 1980. As Forno is an authentic trattoria known best for its Grilled Pizza and Clams al Forno. Whether you are spending days or just and afternoon in Providence, Al Forno is a must!

Location: Providence

Middle Ground Must-Dos

Avenue N America Kitchen

Avenue N has been serving up casual American fare in Rumford Center, the complex where Rumford Baking Powder was once made, since 2011. The seasonally changing menu keeps Rhode Islanders eager to keep going back for more! In the warmer months, patrons can enjoy a cocktail from the carefully curated drink menu on a beautiful patio opens up in the back of the restaurant. Avenue N always makes it worth the visit!

Location: Rumford

Casual Cuisine

Caserta Pizza

Caserta Pizza has been serving traditional Italian pizza since 1953. Located in RI’s historic Federal Hill neighborhood, Caserta is the place locals and tourists alike frequent for delicious Sicilian style pizza. Don’t let the casual, dated ambiance fool you. The thick, fluffy crust loaded with cheese and their homemade sauce makes it well worth the trip.

Location: Federal Hill
